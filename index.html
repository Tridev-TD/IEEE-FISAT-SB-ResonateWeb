RESONATE

Organzied by IEEE FISAT SB

Innovating for Humanity with Signal Processing is a 3-day IEEE FISAT event where students develop signal processing solutions to humanitarian challenges, featuring training, mentorship, and a hackathon focused on the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

RESONATE

Aims to provide participants with both technical skills and a broader vision of how technology can be a force for good, driving long-term sustainability and solving critical global challenges. Through the integration of signal processing with emerging technologies like IoT, participants will learn how to design and implement systems that can operate efficiently in resource-constrained environments, making their solutions practical and scalable.

Primary Audience

College students from engineering, computer science, and related fields. High school students to encourage early interest in technology and sustainable development.

Demographics

Estimated 300-400 attendees
Open to both IEEE members and non-members

Additional Stakeholders:

Industry professionals, NGOs, academic institutions, IEEE chapters.
Mentors, judges, and speakers from the industry and social organizations.

Discover Resonate

Resonate is a dynamic 3-day event organized by the IEEE FISAT Student Branch, aimed at addressing pressing humanitarian challenges through innovative signal processing solutions. Focusing on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the event combines hands-on training, mentorship, and a hackathon, uniting college and high school students nationwide to develop technological solutions to real-world problems within a limited timeframe.

01

Tech4Good

Resonate leverages technology to develop innovative solutions for humanitarian challenges. Focusing on signal processing in healthcare, education, and sustainability, this hackathon invites teams to brainstorm and prototype impactful projects that address real-world problems.

02

Awareness & Outreach

Resonate will showcase how technology supports humanitarian work and the UN SDGs through workshops, talks, and training. Participants will explore global challenges and discover ways to contribute, while outreach to communities and media will extend the event’s impact.

03

Best Practices & Frameworks

Resonate will showcase best practices in using signal processing for humanitarian work. Through documented projects, lessons learned, and expert-led sessions, participants will gain practical frameworks and tools to apply technology for social good.

04

Partnerships & Collaboration

Resonate will build partnerships among students, industry, NGOs, and academia by involving external mentors, judges, and speakers. These connections will spark collaborations, shared initiatives, and potential internships, extending the event’s impact beyond its duration..

Goals

United Nations Sustainable development Goals followed for Resonate

Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being

Projects will address health-related challenges using signal processing technologies, such as remote healthcare, medical diagnostics, and health monitoring solutions.

Learn More

Goal 4: Quality Education

The event will encourage the development of educational tools that use signal processing to enhance learning, especially in underserved communities.

Goal 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Participants will be guided to develop innovative solutions that drive technological advancements in various industries, particularly in sustainable infrastructure and innovation.

Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Solutions will focus on building resilient and sustainable cities, improving infrastructure, and using signal processing for disaster preparedness and response.

Goal 13: Climate Action

The event will promote projects aimed at addressing climate change, such as environmental monitoring, pollution tracking, and disaster mitigation.

Goal 17: Partnerships for the GoalsMarketing Strategy

The event aims to foster collaborations between technical experts, NGOs, and other organizations working toward sustainable development, emphasizing the importance of partnerships to achieve these goals.

Impact

Extending Resonate’s Influence Beyond the Event

How will Resonate empower you to tackle humanitarian challenges?

Inclusivity and Diversity

The event will actively promote inclusivity by welcoming participants from diverse backgrounds, including underrepresented groups in technology. We aim to create a supportive environment that encourages all participants to share their ideas and perspectives.

Networking Opportunities:

Participants will have the chance to connect with industry professionals, experts, and peers, fostering valuable relationships that could lead to future collaborations, internships, and job opportunities.

Sustainability Focus

Each project developed during the hackathon will be evaluated not only on its technical merit but also on its potential social and environmental impact, ensuring that the solutions contribute to long-term sustainability.

Post-Event Support

We plan to provide ongoing support for the best projects developed during Resonate, potentially connecting participants with NGOs or industry partners who can help implement their solutions.
